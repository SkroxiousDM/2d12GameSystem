# Druidic Spellbook

Druidic magic is rooted in a faith and legend cycle that emphasises a deep connection to the land and the forces of nature, drawing on the mysterious and wilful powers that dwell in all places and things. Historically, Druidic practitioners passed lore through the spoken word alone — but this mystical tradition has begun to employ a system of sacred markings called Ogham writing, increasingly woven into Druidic magical rites. Druids are versed in the arts of healing and protective magic, but they are equally capable of calling upon fearsome destructive natural forces to smite their foes.

---

## **Bounties of the Dagda**

Like the mystical cauldron of the Hibernian druid-god after which it is named, this enchantment nourishes and replenishes all creatures within reach of its wondrous power. Shed blood is restored, wounds knit, and bones healed — and it is said that even the newly dead may rise to fight again, long after they were thought to have perished.

* **Skill:** Medicine  
* **Difficulty:** 2  
* **Cost:** 4 Drain  
* **Duration:** Instant  
* **Category:** Ward spell

**Spell Effect:** All allies within Close range immediately remove stress equal to the druid's power rating.

**Success Levels:**

* For 1 Success Level, all defeated allies within range immediately return from defeat.  
* For 2 Success Levels, all affected allies remove a single injury (physical or mental).  
* For 2 Success Levels, the spell affects allies within Medium range instead.

## **Cyclone of Cernunnos**

Focusing their will into a whirling vortex, the druid releases it toward their enemies, where it may wreak immense destruction. This swirling whirlwind is especially harmful to Mythos creatures and entities, dealing damage well beyond its already devastating effects on mortal foes. Yet as the cyclone moves farther from the druid, control begins to slip — the vortex shifts and veers unpredictably before blowing itself out.

* **Skill:** Survival  
* **Difficulty:** 2  
* **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1  
* **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster's power rating  
* **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures a cyclone of wind into a zone within Medium range. The cyclone acts on its own turn in the action order and cannot be attacked or damaged by any means. When first cast, and at the start of the cyclone's turn, it inflicts power \+1 physical damage with the Piercing 1 and Stun damage effects on all creatures and damageable objects within Close range, then moves to an adjacent zone. Damage inflicted on supernatural creatures also gains the Vicious damage effect. When the cyclone moves, the spellcaster may choose its direction if they are within Medium range of it. If the spellcaster is farther than Medium range, the cyclone remains stationary; the GM may spend 1 Doom to move it instead.

**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell grants the spellcaster no control over the cyclone, and the GM need not spend Doom to move it.

**Success Levels:** The spellcaster may increase the damage the cyclone inflicts by \+1 per Success Level spent.

## **Gaze of Balor**

Drawing on the force of the Fomorian king, this baleful gaze strikes fear and terror into the hearts of its targets. Extending from the druid in an enervating arc, the Gaze of Balor causes its victim's knees to buckle, limbs to tremble, and soul to quail — an afflicted target is liable to throw down their arms, turn tail, and flee gibbering, heedless of the consequences.

* **Skill:** Persuasion  
* **Difficulty:** 2, opposed by the target's Will \+ Resilience  
* **Cost:** 5 Drain, Stun  
* **Duration:** Instant  
* **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** Targeting a single enemy within Medium range, the Gaze of Balor inflicts power \+2 mental damage with the Intense damage effect.

**Success Levels:**

* For 2 Success Levels, add the Drain damage effect.  
* For 2 Success Levels, add the Persistent 6 damage effect.

## **Gift of Arduinna**

Those who call upon the powers of the lady of Arduenna Silva — the Forest of Ardennes — are truly blessed. They move with preternatural swiftness, shrug off fatigue, and perform far beyond their normal limits in combat. They pass through wooded terrain, over broken ground, and across streams as though unhindered.

* **Skill:** Survival  
* **Difficulty:** 3  
* **Cost:** 4 Drain  
* **Duration:** A total number of rounds equal to the spellcaster's power rating, divided evenly among all affected targets (rounding all fractions down)  
* **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell may affect the spellcaster and any allies within Close range, up to a total number of targets equal to the spellcaster's power rating. For the duration of the spell, affected targets may move up to 1 additional zone whenever they move as a minor or major action, and may take one additional minor action for free each turn.

**Success Levels:** For every 2 Success Levels spent, the spellcaster or a single affected ally may take one additional major action during one of their own turns, provided that turn falls before the spell's duration ends. No character may gain more than one additional major action this way.

## **Horn of Néit**

Channelling a fearsome curse from the Druidic god of war, the druid summons a mystical instrument and plays a song that turns the very earth against its victims. Phantom roots, tendrils, and branches snake out to ensnare limbs, holding targets fast and leaving them hapless before the wielder's power. Those trapped by the Horn of Néit can neither move nor act until the spell fades.

* **Skill:** Survival  
* **Difficulty:** 1  
* **Cost:** 4 Drain  
* **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster's power rating  
* **Category:** Curse spell

**Spell Effect:** This spell targets all enemy creatures within Medium range. The spellcaster rolls a number of dice equal to their power rating and compares the number of effects rolled against each target's ranks in Athletics. For targets larger than a human, add that target's scale to their Athletics ranks. Any creature whose Athletics is lower than the number of effects rolled suffers the Snare damage effect and cannot take any physical actions other than attempting to break free. Breaking free requires passing an Agility \+ Athletics or Brawn \+ Athletics test with a difficulty equal to the number of effects rolled. Ensnared creatures are freed automatically when the spell ends.

**Success Levels:**

* For each Success Level spent, one additional creature within Long range is also affected.  
* For each Success Level spent, the spellcaster rolls \+1 die.

## **The Ogham Sign**

Drawn from the sacred alphabet, the Ogham Sign is a devastating weapon against Mythos creatures — dealing enormous damage upon release and significantly weakening those that manage to survive its initial onslaught.

* **Skill:** Academia  
* **Difficulty:** 3  
* **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 1  
* **Duration:** Instant  
* **Category:** Banishment spell

**Spell Effect:** This spell targets a single supernatural creature within Medium range, inflicting power \+2 mental damage with the Drain and Piercing 2 damage effects — even if the creature is normally immune to mental attacks (treat such creatures as having a Courage of 6). If the creature suffers one or more mental injuries from this spell, it loses the benefits of its Invulnerable special rule for the remainder of the current scene. If the creature is defeated by the spell, it vanishes, banished to its realm of origin.

**Success Levels:**

* For 2 Success Levels, add the Intense damage effect.  
* For 2 Success Levels, add the Stun damage effect.

## **Roots of the Earth**

Tapping into the ancient byways and hidden roots of the world — known only to the inhabitants of the hollow hills — the spellcaster transports themselves and their companions to a nearby location, vanishing as though swallowed by the earth itself before reappearing wherever they will, even behind an enemy formation.

* **Skill:** Survival  
* **Difficulty:** 2  
* **Cost:** 3 Drain, Stun  
* **Duration:** Instant  
* **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell may affect the spellcaster and any allies within Close range, up to a total number of targets equal to half the spellcaster's power rating (rounding up). Affected characters vanish and instantly reappear at any point within Long range that is within line of sight.

**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, all transported characters gain \+2 Cover resistance until the start of the spellcaster's next turn.

## **Spear of Lug**

The druid focuses their will and calls upon the power of Lug, the Druidic god, combining their energies to form a living weapon — a burnished spear of destructive force hurled at foes to inflict immense damage. Directed by the druid's will alone, the spear homes inexorably in on its target, slicing through obstacles and ignoring all cover and armour to find its mark.

* **Skill:** Fighting  
* **Difficulty:** 3  
* **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 1  
* **Duration:** Instant  
* **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy or object within Medium range, inflicting power \+2 physical damage with the Piercing 3 damage effect.

**Success Levels:**

* For 2 Success Levels, add the Intense damage effect.  
* For 2 Success Levels, add the Vicious damage effect.
