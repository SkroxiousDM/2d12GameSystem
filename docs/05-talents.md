# Talents

Talents are the defining features and abilities that set a hero apart from the common run of humanity. Many Talents have Requirements—certain Skill Ranks, Attribute scores, or other Talents—that must be met before they can be learned. Losing the ability to meet a Requirement means losing the Talent.

Talents provide both **active** and **passive** benefits. Passive benefits persistently modify Attributes, Skills, or other game elements. Many Talents also grant access to **Actions**, **Reactions**, and **Desperate Actions**.

A character earns Talents during character creation through their Role, and potentially through their Culture and Background. After character creation, a character may spend 20 XP to purchase a new Talent, provided all Requirements are met.

Requirements typically include:

* An Attribute above or below a certain value, such as Agility 4 or fewer than 2 ranks in Reason
* A number of ranks in a Skill, such as Rank 2 Fighting
* A specialization, such as Fighting (Melee Weapons)
* The absence of ranks in a specific Skill or specialization
* Some Talents may only be taken at character creation
* Some Talents prevent or require taking other Talents

# Combat Talents

### Dangerous

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Fighting*

* **Desperate Action:** Make a Fighting or Archery Skill Check as though you had Rank 5. If your Fighting or Archery is already Rank 5, this attack affects two characters; make a single Skill Check.
* **Desperate Action:** You may take one additional Reaction this turn.

---

### Deadly

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding only a single one-handed weapon. This bonus is retained while also carrying a shield.
* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +3 with one-handed weapons. Holding a shield or dual wielding reduces this bonus by 1.

---

### Scrapper

*Prerequisites: Might 3 or Dexterity 3, Rank 1 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When Unarmed and you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +3.
* **Benefit:** When you spend Tychē to gain Advantage on an Unarmed attack, that attack inflicts Sōma Damage as though you were armed.

---

### Agile Attacks

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3, Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** You may use your Dexterity instead of your Might when calculating damage for melee weapons with the Subtle trait.

---

### Charger

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound and have no current Sōma Damage, you may increase its rolled severity by +2.
* **Benefit:** If you move from a non-adjacent position to attack, and you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by an additional +2.
* **Benefit:** You may use Might instead of Agility when calculating Akmē.

---

### Dirty Fighter

*Prerequisites: Might 3 or Dexterity 3, Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When you fail to hit with a Fighting Skill attack, you may make an immediate second attack with Disadvantage.
* **Benefit:** You may choose to make Fighting Skill Checks with Disadvantage. When you succeed on a Fighting Skill Check made with Disadvantage, you add **+2 to the weapon's Damage rating** for that hit (i.e. roll two additional d3, and subtract 2 more, per the standard Damage Resolution in Ch 15).

---

### Reckless

*Prerequisites: Might 3, Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** You may choose to give all your Fighting Skill Checks—and all attacks made against you—Advantage for the round.

---

### Combat Master

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** You count as two combatants for the purpose of determining whether one side outnumbers another in melee.

---

### Disarm

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you make a melee attack against a target your size or smaller, you may choose to disarm rather than deal damage. On a success, the target drops a held item, which lands in a random adjacent zone. If you win by +2 Levels of Success, you choose the zone; if you win by +4 Levels of Success, you may take the item yourself.

---

### Directed Strike

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** You do not suffer Disadvantage when using the Target Location Action with a melee weapon.

---

### Counter Attack

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you are the target of a melee attack and win the Opposed Fighting Check, you may deal damage to your attacker as though you had made the attack yourself.

This Talent may be taken multiple times. You may Counter Attack once per round for each time you have taken this Talent.

---

### Savage

*Prerequisites: Might 3, Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +1 for every untreated Critical Wound you currently carry.
* **Reaction:** When you receive a Critical Wound, you may immediately attack.
* **Desperate Action:** Take 1 Sōma Damage to make an additional Fighting Skill attack this turn.

---

### Agile

*Prerequisite: Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** +1 Armour while unarmoured.
* **Benefit:** +1 Akmē while unarmoured.
* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +1 while unarmoured.
* **Reaction:** If you Wound an unwounded character, you may immediately attack them again with a Fighting Skill Check.

---

### Warrior

*Prerequisite: Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +1.
* **Reaction:** When a character willingly moves away from you on their turn, you may use your Reaction to attack them.
* **Desperate Action:** If you inflict a Critical Wound, you may attack again immediately.

---

### Impassable

*Prerequisite: Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** +4 Armour.
* **Special:** Your Dexterity may not increase your Evasion.

---

### Tough

*Prerequisite: Constitution 4*

* **Benefit:** +1 Sōma.
* **Benefit:** +2 Armour while unarmoured.
* **Desperate Action:** When a single attack would deal more than 1 Critical Wound, reduce it to 1.

---

### Battle Rage

You can work yourself into a furious killing state.

* **Free Action:** Make a Challenging (+0) Willpower Check to enter a rage. While raging, your Might increases by +1 and you are immune to the Frightened condition. On your turn, you must move toward the nearest visible enemy and engage them in melee. The rage ends when all visible enemies are defeated or when you suffer the Stunned or Unconscious condition.

This Talent may be taken multiple times. Each time after the first, choose one of the following:

* **Controlled Fury:** You may make a Difficult (-2) Resilience Check as an Action to end your rage voluntarily.
* **Fearless Rage:** Whenever you would become Frightened, you may immediately enter a rage instead.
* **Relentless Momentum:** Once per turn, when you kill an enemy while raging, you may immediately Move or take one additional Action.

---

### Berserker

*Prerequisites: Battle Rage 2, Constitution 6*

* **Benefit:** When you would receive a Critical Wound that kills you, make a Challenging (+0)  Constitution + Resilience Check. On a success, you do not die and instead regain 1 Sōma. Each subsequent use of this benefit increases the Difficulty by 1 level. A Rest resets the Difficulty.

---

### Dual Wielder

* **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding two swords, daggers, maces, axes, or fighting knives.
* **Benefit:** When you would inflict a Critical Wound while dual wielding, inflict one additional Critical Wound.

---

### Opportunist

*Prerequisites: Dual Wielder, Dexterity 4, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When you successfully Evade while dual wielding swords, daggers, or fighting knives, you may immediately attack your attacker with a Fighting Skill Check.

---

### Bruiser

*Prerequisites: Dual Wielder, Might 4, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When your attack while dual wielding swords, axes, or maces is mitigated by armour, you may make an additional Fighting Skill Check against the same target.

---

### Dervish

*Prerequisites: Opportunist or Bruiser, Might 5 or Dexterity 5*

* **Desperate Action:** While dual wielding, make a single Fighting Skill Check against a number of adjacent characters up to your Dexterity or Might score.

---

### Ironclad

* **Benefit:** Armour requires 1 fewer Stamina to wear. You may forgo this benefit to have all armour grant +1 Armour instead.
* **Benefit:** Armour's Dexterity limit is increased by 1. You may forgo this benefit to have all armour grant +1 Armour instead.

---

### Defender

*Prerequisites: Might 4, Rank 2 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** Shields grant +1 Armour.
* **Reaction:** When you do not move on your turn, you may raise a heavy shield to gain cover for the round, imposing Disadvantage on Archery and Magic Skill Checks made against you.

---

### Shieldbearer

*Prerequisites: Defender, Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When your attack fails to inflict any Wound, you may immediately make an additional shield bash using your Fighting Skill.

---

### Stalwart

*Prerequisites: Shieldbearer, Might 5, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When adjacent to more than one character who has attacked you this round, you may make an attack with your Fighting Skill.
* **Benefit:** If you end your turn adjacent to three or more hostile characters, you gain Advantage on your next Fighting Skill Check.

---

### Vigilant

*Prerequisites: Shieldbearer, Might 5, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When an adjacent character attacks a friendly character adjacent to you, you may immediately attack the attacker with your Fighting Skill.
* **Reaction:** If an adjacent ally without the Vigilant Talent would receive one or more Sōma Damage and you are wielding a shield, you may take those Sōma Damage instead. They are mitigated as though by armour.

---

### Butcher

*Prerequisites: Athletics (Lifting), Might 4, Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding a two-handed axe, mace, or sword.
* **Benefit:** When you would inflict a Critical Wound with a two-handed weapon, inflict one additional Critical Wound.

---

### Cleaver

*Prerequisites: Athletics (Lifting), Might 5, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When you kill a character with a two-handed weapon, you may immediately attack a character adjacent to them.

---

### Manslayer

*Prerequisites: Cleaver, Might 6*

* **Benefit:** You may spend 2 SL from Cleaver's Fighting Skill Check to attack an additional character adjacent to the first.

---

### Lasher

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3, Rank 1 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding a spear or staff in both hands.
* **Reaction:** If you were not adjacent to any enemy on your last turn, gain Advantage on your first Fighting Skill Check this turn with a two-handed spear or staff.

---

### Staver

*Prerequisites: Lasher, Dexterity 4, Rank 3 Fighting*

* **Reaction:** When you would make a Fighting Skill Check with Advantage using a two-handed spear or staff, you may instead make a single attack against two adjacent characters.

---

### Pole Sweep

*Prerequisites: Staver, Dexterity 5, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Desperate Action:** Spend one Tychē to make a single Fighting Skill Check with a two-handed spear or staff against any number of adjacent characters.

---

### Duelist

*Prerequisites: Reason 3, Rank 1 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you would make a Fighting Skill Check with Advantage, roll one additional die and take the best results.
* **Benefit:** When adjacent to only one other character and you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +2.

---

### Skirmisher

*Prerequisites: Duelist, Reason 4, Rank 5 Fighting*

* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +1.
* **Benefit:** Once per round, when you use a Reaction, you may use one Reaction you have already used this round.

---

### Hound

* **Benefit:** Choose one character. You gain Advantage on all Athletics Checks made to reach them, so long as they are no farther than Far range. This lasts until you take a Rest or designate a new target.
* **Desperate Action:** Gain +1 SL on one attack against your designated target.

---

### Archer

*Prerequisite: Fighting (Archery) specialization*

* **Benefit:** +1 Akmē if your bow is readied.
* **Benefit:** +1 Evasion while wielding a bow.
* **Benefit:** If you do not move on your turn, Critical Wounds inflicted by your Fighting (Archery) Skill inflict one additional Critical Wound.

---

### Deadshot

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Fighting (Archery) or (Thrown Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** When you Aim and make a ranged attack, you gain +1 Level of Success. If you take this Talent again, you also have Advantage on Checks to Target a specific Location when aiming.

---

### Marksman

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3, Rank 3 Fighting (Archery) or (Thrown Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** When you deal a Critical Hit or Critical Wound, you may increase its rolled severity by +2 with Fighting (Archery) Skill Checks.
* **Benefit:** You may make Fighting (Archery) Skill Checks against Distant characters without Disadvantage.
* **Benefit:** Partial cover does not impose Disadvantage on your Fighting (Archery) Skill Checks.

---

### Warden

*Prerequisites: Marksman, Dexterity 4, Rank 5 Fighting (Archery) or (Thrown Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** +2 Evasion while wielding a bow.
* **Benefit:** You no longer suffer Disadvantage on Fighting (Archery) Skill Checks made against Adjacent characters.
* **Reaction:** After attacking a Far or Distant character, you may make an additional Fighting (Archery) Skill Check against a Near or Adjacent character.

---

### Ranger

*Prerequisites: Marksman, Dexterity 4, Rank 5 Fighting (Archery) or (Thrown Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** Fighting (Archery) Skill Checks made from a hidden position with bows, crossbows, or thrown weapons do not automatically reveal your location.
* **Reaction:** If you make a Fighting (Archery) Skill Check against a Distant character and inflict a Critical Wound, you may attack a character adjacent to them.

---

### Flanking Shot

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting (Archery or Thrown Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** When you make a ranged attack targeting an enemy in the same zone as a visible ally, the cover your target benefits from is reduced by 2 Armour.

---

### Ambidextrous

* **Benefit:** You may wield a one-handed melee weapon and a thrown weapon or short blade simultaneously without penalty to either.

---

### Thrower

* **Benefit:** You may use your Thievery Skill to throw knives, hatchets, or hand axes as attacks against Adjacent or Near characters.
* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Thievery Skill Checks to conceal small blades on your person.
* **Desperate Action:** Gain +1 SL on a Thievery Skill Check to throw a blade or small axe.

---

### Brawler's Cunning

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting (Unarmed)*

* **Benefit:** When you deal damage with a melee attack, you may choose to inflict the Blinded or Deafened condition. If you succeed by +3 Levels of Success or more, you may inflict the Stunned condition instead.

---

### Bone Breaker

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 in a Medicae specialization*

* **Benefit:** As an Action, you may break a bone of a Restrained target for whom you have the relevant Medicae specialization. Choose a Hit Location: it gains a Broken Bone (Minor) condition. If it already has a Broken Bone (Minor), it becomes a Broken Bone (Major) instead.

---

### Two-Handed Cleave

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting (Two-handed Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** When you wield a two-handed melee weapon, it gains the Spread trait, and attacks you make with it cannot strike you.

This Talent may be taken multiple times. Each time after the first, choose one of the following:

* **Brutalist:** The weapon also gains the Rend (+1) trait.
* **Leg Sweep:** When you hit a target in the leg, you may halve the damage to force them to make a Hard (-4) Reflexes (Balance) Check or fall Prone.

---

### Fencer

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting (Melee Weapons)*

* **Benefit:** You treat all one-handed melee weapons that lack the Subtle trait as having the Defensive trait.

This Talent may be taken multiple times. Each time after the first, choose one of the following:

* **Fencer Feint:** When you successfully attack with a one-handed melee weapon, you may choose to deal no damage and instead impose Disadvantage on the target's attacks against you until the end of your next turn.
* **Fencer Guard:** When you make an attack with a one-handed melee weapon, you may choose to make that attack with Disadvantage in order to simultaneously take the Dodge Action.

---

### Exploit Vulnerability

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Observation (Instincts)*

* **Benefit:** If you spend time studying a target before combat, you may choose the Hit Location of your first successful attack against them.

---

### Hatred

You carry a deep and consuming hatred for a particular group. Choose the object of your hatred.

* **Benefit:** Whenever you encounter them, make a Difficult (-2) Resilience (Discipline) Check. If you fail, you have Advantage on Resilience (Fear) Checks against them and gain +1 Level of Success on attack Checks targeting them, but you must pursue and destroy them by the most direct means available. You may repeat the Discipline Check at the end of each of your turns to regain control. The effect ends when you fall unconscious or all visible targets of your hatred are dead.

---

### Shock Assault

*Prerequisites: Agility 4, Rank 3 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** You gain 2 additional Actions on your turn during a Surprise Round.

---

# Command & Leadership Talents

### Tactician

*Prerequisites: Charisma 3, Reason 3*

* **Benefit:** At any point you could make an attack, you may instead forgo it to allow a Near character to use their Reaction to attack. If you do not move, they may also move a Near distance.
* **Benefit:** You may use your Academia Skill to assess and recall information about battle tactics, military strategy, and campaign logistics.

---

### Leader

*Prerequisites: Tactician, Charisma 4 or Reason 4*

* **Benefit:** When you spend Tychē to grant Advantage to another character, you may instead grant it to up to two characters simultaneously.
* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL when using your Academia Skill to assess military tactics, strategy, and logistics.
* **Desperate Action:** If your Reason or Charisma is 6 or greater, you may award another character Tychē in the same manner as the GM.

---

### Inspiring Presence

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Manipulation (Rhetoric) or Rank 2 Tactics (Leadership)*

* **Benefit:** Allies in your zone who can see you gain Advantage on Resilience (Discipline) Checks.

---

### Unflinching Presence

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Tactics (Leadership)*

* **Benefit:** Whenever your side would lose Superiority, make a Hard (-4) Manipulation (Leadership) Check. On a success, Superiority is not lost.

---

### Superior Commander

*Prerequisite: Rank 4 Tactics (Leadership)*

* **Benefit:** As an Action, choose a number of allies equal to your current Superiority. Each may immediately take one Action. No character may benefit from this Talent more than once per round.

---

### Overseer

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Tactics (Leadership)*

* **Benefit:** As an Action, make a Challenging (+0) Tactics (Leadership) Check. On a success, your target may use your Skill level instead of their own for their next Check.

---

### Drilled

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Resilience (Discipline)*

* **Benefit:** You have Advantage on Checks to resist the Frightened condition, and gain +1 Level of Success on Resilience (Discipline) Checks for each ally with the Drilled Talent in the same zone.

If another party member also has Drilled, both of you gain the following:

* **Ad Fulco:** When in the same zone as another character with Drilled, you may act immediately after them in Initiative and gain +1 Level of Success on all Checks that round.

---

### Guardian

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Reflexes*

* **Benefit:** At the start of combat, as a Free Action, designate one allied character within Medium Range as your ward. If that character is attacked and you are within Medium Range, you may immediately move to Defend them. If doing so breaks your engagement, opponents have Disadvantage on their melee attack.

---

### Bulwark

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Reflexes*

* **Benefit:** When you take the Defend Action while carrying a shield, you may choose to extend your defence to one ally within Immediate Range, or to both that ally and the zone you currently occupy.

---

### Coup de Grâce

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Reflexes*

* **Benefit:** If you have 2 or more Superiority, you may take 2 turns in a row, sacrificing your turn next round to do so.

---

### Martyr

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Resilience (Fortitude)*

* **Benefit:** If you have at least one untreated Critical Wound received in service to your cause, you gain Levels of Success on Checks equal to your Willpower. Only Checks made to directly confront enemies may benefit from this bonus.

---

### Distracting

* **Benefit:** Once per encounter, you may interrupt another character making a Check, imposing Disadvantage. In combat, this Talent is used as a Reaction.

This Talent may be taken a second time; if so, choose one of the following:

* **Centre of Attention:** When within Immediate Range of a non-ally, you may impose Disadvantage on Checks that target makes toward anyone other than you.
* **Stunning Interjection:** Once per encounter when you use this Talent, your target is Stunned (Minor) until the end of their next turn.

---

# Mobility & Athleticism Talents

### Light-Footed

*Prerequisite: Dexterity 4*

* **Benefit:** +2 Akmē.
* **Benefit:** +1 Evasion while wielding neither weapons nor shields.

---

### Athletic

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 5 or Might 5, Light-Footed*

* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on all Dexterity-based Checks to jump, climb, and tumble.
* **Benefit:** Reduce falling damage by half as though mitigated by armour.
* **Desperate Action:** Prevent up to 2 Critical Wounds from a single fall.

---

### Nimble

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 6, Light-Footed*

* **Desperate Action:** All attacks against you have Disadvantage for the round.
* **Desperate Action:** Halve the Sōma Damage from one attack that hits you, as though by armour.

---

### Adrenaline

* **Benefit:** If you have a Critical Wound, your Speed increases by one step until the end of the encounter, and you gain Advantage on Athletics Checks made during a Pursuit or while Fleeing.

---

### Contortionist

* **Benefit:** You may fit into unusually cramped passages. You also have Advantage on Checks to resist or escape the Restrained condition or a grapple.

---

### Sure-Footed

*Prerequisites: Rank 1 Athletics, Rank 1 Resilience (Fortitude), Rank 1 Reflexes*

* **Benefit:** If you have at least 1 point of Superiority, you become Fast. You have Advantage on Checks to resist or end the Prone condition, may stand from Prone as a Free Action once per turn if not Restrained, and have Advantage on Checks to resist being forcibly moved.

---

### Hit and Run

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Reflexes (Acrobatics)*

* **Benefit:** As a Free Action, whenever you deal one or more Sōma Damage or Critical Wounds, you may make a Reflexes (Acrobatics) Check to Disengage and move to an adjacent zone.

---

### Slippery

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Reflexes*

- **Benefit:** You may Take Cover as a Free Action.
- **Benefit:** When you break from engagement and an opponent makes an immediate attack against you, they have Disadvantage on that attack.
- **Benefit:** If you take this Talent twice, you may Disengage as a Free Action.

---

### Porter

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Athletics (Lifting)*

* **Benefit:** Each Stamina point you invest in carrying counts double for the purposes of encumbrance.

---

### Familiar Terrain

You know the land of your upbringing the way others know their own face. This Talent may be taken multiple times, choosing a new environment each time.

* **Benefit:** Choose one of the following environments. Within that environment, you have Advantage on Stealth Checks and gain +1 Armour from Cover beyond what cover normally provides.
  * **Agricultural:** Farmed fields, orchards, pastures, fishing villages
  * **Forest:** Deep woodland, managed hunting grounds, ancient groves
  * **Highlands:** Mountains, hill country, exposed moorland
  * **Maritime:** Coastlines, harbours, river deltas, shallow water
  * **Ruins:** Abandoned settlements, derelict forts, overgrown infrastructure
  * **Settlement:** Towns, cities, marketplaces, densely populated areas
  * **Wasteland:** Marshes, tundra, desert, and other uninhabited wilderness
  * **War Zone:** Active battlefields, siege works, military encampments, recently contested ground

# Stealth & Skullduggery Talents

### Subtle

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Stealth Checks to blend into crowds.
* **Benefit:** Survival Skill Checks made to track you have Disadvantage.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Stealth Skill one Rank.

---

### Cutthroat

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** Critical Wounds resulting from Surprise Attacks made with Advantage inflict one additional Critical Wound.
* **Benefit:** Your killing blows are silent.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Stealth Skill one Rank.

---

### Ambusher

*Prerequisites: Cutthroat, Rank 5 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** Critical Wounds from Surprise Attacks against characters who have not yet acted this round reduce their Akmē to zero for the round.
* **Benefit:** You may choose to make any of your killing blows silent.

---

### Unseen

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 5, Rank 5 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Stealth Skill Checks if you move only to an adjacent location on your turn.
* **Benefit:** You may spend one Tychē to reroll a Stealth Skill Check.

---

### Quick-Fingered

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3, Rank 1 Thievery*

* **Benefit:** Once per Thievery Skill Check to pick pockets or perform sleight of hand, you may reroll all dice.
* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on all Thievery Checks involving coins, rings, dice, and jewelry.
* **Benefit:** If you would fail a Skill Check to win at a game of chance, you may cheat by making a Thievery Skill Check at Advantage and using that result instead.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Thievery Skill one Rank.

---

### Burglar

*Prerequisites: Reason 3, Rank 5 Thievery, Lockpick specialization*

* **Benefit:** You can always improvise lock-picking tools from materials at hand, regardless of equipment carried.
* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Thievery Skill Checks to pick locks when you have proper picks.
* **Benefit:** Once you have opened a lock, you automatically succeed at all future Thievery Skill Checks to open it again.

---

### Skulker

*Prerequisites: Rank 1 Survival (Tracking), Rank 1 Stealth*

* **Benefit:** When using Survival (Tracking) to shadow a target, you need not make Stealth Checks to remain undetected—unless you do something to attract attention through carelessness or misfortune.

---

### False Retreat

*Prerequisite: Rank 3 Manipulation (Deception)*

* **Benefit:** As an Action, you may pretend to flee. Reduce Superiority by 1 and remove yourself from the encounter. At the start of your next turn, you may reappear at any location within Long Range of your previous position, hidden from enemies. When you re-enter the encounter, Superiority increases by 1.

---

# Social & Influence Talents

### Liar

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Manipulation*

* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on all Manipulation Skill Checks intended to deceive through deliberate falsehood.
* **Benefit:** Characters who have not previously met you have Disadvantage on their first Insight – Manipulation Skill Check made against you.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.

---

### Alluring

*Prerequisites: Charisma 3, Rank 1 Manipulation*

* **Benefit:** You may spend one Tychē to gain Advantage on all Manipulation Skill Checks against a single character until either of you completes a Rest.
* **Benefit:** +1 Charisma.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.
* **Desperate Action:** Gain +1 SL on one Manipulation or Performance Skill Check.

---

### Mercantile

*Prerequisites: Reason 3 or Charisma 3, Rank 1 Manipulation*

* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Academia Skill Checks to identify or appraise goods and objects.
* **Benefit:** Gain +1 SL on Manipulation and Insight Checks for negotiating prices and arranging deals.
* **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.

---

### Air of Authority

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Manipulation*

* **Benefit:** The first time you encounter a new group, you may make a Challenging (+0) Manipulation Check. On a success, you gain +1 Influence with that group until the end of the session.

---

### Anointed

This power is not a reward for belief — it is the patron spending, through you, a portion of the magical potential it skims from your congregation's rites. A cynic in good standing wields it; a sincere outsider with no patron does not.

* **Benefit:** Once per session, you may add your Willpower in Levels of Success to a Check directly tied to your office and patron, such as invoking divine judgement during an interrogation, crying out a battle blessing as you strike down a creature of darkness, or petitioning your patron for guidance when searching for a holy relic.

**Saints and Miracles** *(requires GM permission)*

* **Benefit — Burning Tychē:** You may burn a Tychē point to perform one great and inexplicable act—restoring the apparently dead, holding back a collapsing structure, or calming a panicked crowd by presence alone. If you have no Tychē remaining, you may still perform one final act of this kind, at the cost of your life.
