# **Spellbook of Yog-Sothoth**

Yog-Sothoth, the Outer God, is both keeper of all knowledge and coterminous with all time and space, and so its magic is based on unlocking gateways, freeing forbidden knowledge, and revealing the hidden pathways that open believers’ eyes to the true nature of reality. Unlike Nyarlathotep, Yog-Sothoth is not malign in the traditional sense, but welcomes those who would gaze upon the true state of the multiverse, even if it might drive them insane. Even though one aspect of it is trapped with the black sun, the god sometimes manifests itself as a succession of gleaming vortexes and spheres. Occasionally it has been known to interfere in the affairs of mortals, impregnating them to create monstrous hybrids.

---

## **Binding of Yog-Sothoth**

Calling on the chaotic aspects of the Outer God to confuse and mislead, the sorcerer binds all enemies nearby with this spell. While it does no direct physical damage, those who succumb to its deleterious effects are overcome, becoming rooted to the spot, unable to move, attack, or defend themselves.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** All enemies within Close range suffer Power +2 mental Stress with the Snare effect. Enemies who would suffer one or more injuries from this spell are paralyzed instead of suffering injuries. Paralyzed enemies may not take any action other than to try to break free, requiring a successful Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Difficult (-2).
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures within Close range, not just enemies.

**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 1 effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Persistent 3 damage effect; the Persistent damage also has the Snare effect.

## **Foetid Touch of Aforgomon**

Sometimes the Outer God can be persuaded to “favor” mortals with its direct touch, impregnating them with its unholy seed. Some may be able to resist these foul effects, but if possessed, they slowly begin to transform into the fiendish Bloodborn.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2), opposed by the target’s Agility + Fighting (Difficulty Difficult (-2))
- **Cost:** 7 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Reach. If the spell is successfully cast, the target suffers Power +1 physical Stress with the Persistent 3 effect. Any character Defeated by this spell immediately starts dying, and if they die, a Bloodborn emerges from the character’s remains in the following round.

**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect, applied to both the initial Stress and any Stress caused by the Persistent effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense effect, which applies to both the initial Stress and any caused by the Persistent effect.

## **Gaze of the Multiverse**

Yog-Sothoth is the key and the gate, the keeper of hidden knowledge. Using this spell, the spellcaster opens the victim’s eyes to the true nature of the cold, harsh, unyielding universe, which has the nuclear chaos of the mad, blind god Azathoth at its center. Those affected by Gaze of the Multiverse are overcome by the overwhelming nature of this revelation and lose coherence and focus for its duration.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** All enemies within Close range of the spellcaster suffer Power +2 mental Stress, with the Stun effect.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures within Close range, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Drain effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Vicious effect.

---

## **Loathsome Bond of 'Umr At-Tawil**

Yog-Sothoth is the warden of forbidden knowledge and this fearsome spell is aimed at breaking a foe’s morale. Using this loathsome incantation, the sorcerer not only does substantial harm, but siphons off a portion of their foe’s life force to replenish their own.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets each enemy within Close range and inflicts Power +1 mental Stress with the Drain and Piercing 1 effects. In addition, if the spell inflicts one or more injuries, the spellcaster may remove one of their own injuries.
**Flawed:** If a flawed version of this spell is cast, it targets every creature within Close range, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spellcaster removes one injury for each injury inflicted.

---

## **Way of the Gatekeeper**

Yog-Sothoth is the opener of the ways, and guardian of the hidden paths, and casters are able to draw on this forbidden knowledge to move themselves and their allies swiftly and silently across the battlefield, sometimes appearing deep behind enemy lines, or using these hidden dimensional byways to evade imminent danger.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster transports a number of willing allies in Close range equal to half their Power, rounding up; this may include or exclude themselves. The affected characters vanish and reappear in any zone within Long range.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the maximum number of affected allies is equal to the spellcaster’s Power, rather than only half.
- For 2 Success Levels, the affected characters may reappear in a location within a mile which the spellcaster is familiar with, provided they have spent at least an hour in that location previously.

## **Spheres of Yog-Sothoth**

The sorcerer summons a small portion of Yog-Sothoth’s being to this plane, in the form of a series of ephemeral spheres glowing with a pallid unholy light, then directs them at their enemies who wilt under the Outer God’s unclean touch.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Varies, see below
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures one, two, or three spheres, which the spellcaster hurls at enemies within Medium range. The difficulty of the spell is equal to the number of spheres summoned. Each sphere targets a different enemy, and each enemy hit suffers Power +2 physical Stress with the Drain and Stun effects.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect.
