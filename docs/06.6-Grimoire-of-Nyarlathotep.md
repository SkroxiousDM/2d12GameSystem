# **Grimoire of Nyarlathotep**

The Messenger of the Outer Gods confers ominous boons upon his children and worshippers, sowing illusion, deception, delusion, and death. Those worshippers who wield Nyarlathotep’s unholy magics do so at great cost to their own personal health and rationality.

Drawn from the power of the Dreamlands, the battlefield spells contained in the Grimoire of Nyarlathotep are among the foulest sorceries practiced by any faction in the Hidden Wars. The Crawling Chaos delights to see them inflicted upon friend and foe alike.

---

## **Delirium of the Dark Pharaoh**

Tapping into the sprawling chaos of the Dreamlands, the spellcaster calls forth the power of the Crawling Chaos, conjuring horrific visions and glimpses of strange dimensions and eerie planes beyond this earthly existence.

Characters find their willpower stretched to breaking point, and any who are unable to resist are gripped in an unearthly fervor. Delirious, they turn against each other or nearby allies until their temporary frenzy abates.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single creature within Medium range. The target suffers Power +1 mental Stress with the Persistent 5 damage effect. If this spell would otherwise inflict one or more injuries on its target, either from the mental damage dealt or from the Persistent damage effect, then instead of sustaining injuries, the target is driven to a violent frenzy and attacks the nearest creature, friend or foe, without thought of pain or danger. This frenzy ends at the end of the scene or until the target is Defeated.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, both the initial damage roll and subsequent rolls required by the Persistent damage effect gain the Piercing 1 damage effect.
- For every 2 Success Levels spent, the spell affects one additional target.

## **Lesser Black Rain**

Adapted from ancient Hyperborean magic, this spell summons a swirling maelstrom of lightning-flecked, thunderous black clouds. Once the maelstrom has formed, the spell cannot be halted, and soon after, the vortex rains down razor-sharp shards of jagged obsidian on any victims not under hard cover.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster selects a zone they can perceive within Long range, and a menacing storm gathers above that zone. The storm cannot be attacked or damaged in any way, and it receives its own turn in the action order. The storm does nothing on its first turn after being created, but on each of its subsequent turns, it inflicts Power physical damage with the Piercing 1 damage effect on all creatures and damageable objects within its zone that are not within hard cover, a ceiling or object that fully impedes an attack.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, add the Vicious damage effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, move the storm to any adjacent zone as a major action, requiring a Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0).

## **Mask of the Faceless Sphinx**

Calling on the power of the Crawling Chaos, the spellcaster invokes blessings for their allies, who gain the ability to cloak themselves in the aspect of one of the God of a Thousand Forms’ most inscrutable incarnations, the Faceless Sphinx. While under this protection, their outlines seem to shift and shimmer, making them much harder to hit, while any damage they take is much reduced.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Persistent 3
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster and any allies within Close range when the spell is cast become harder to hit, increasing the difficulty of attacks against them by +1. Further, they count as having heavy cover.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the difficulty increase for attacks against the affected characters increases to +2.
- For 2 Success Levels, affected characters receive +4 Cover resistance instead of the +2 Cover resistance normally granted by heavy cover.
- For 3 Success Levels, any ranged or melee attack that misses an affected character inflicts 4 mental damage with the Stun damage effect upon the attacker.

## **Rays of the Black Sun (Lesser Dread Equinox)**

Evoking the power of chaos and confusion, the spellcaster draws down and directs a scintilla of the rays of the black sun directly at the enemy.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Medium range. The target suffers Power +2 mental damage with the Piercing 1 and Snare damage effects.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Persistent 4 damage effect.
- The spell may affect one additional target for every 2 Success Levels spent.

---

## **The Nameless Mists**

The spellcaster summons a small portion of the Dark Pharaoh’s unholy sibling, the Nameless Mist, to Earth. There, it manifests as a restless, shifting, chaotic mist, lingering and burning with a horrible internal intensity, damaging anyone caught in its unholy tendrils.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures a foul mist that fills the spellcaster’s current zone. Any enemy within the mist at the end of their turn suffers mental damage equal to the spellcaster’s Power +1, with the Piercing 2 and Stun damage effects. In addition, the zone gains a location truth, Shifting, Vile Mists, that hinders vision into, out of, and through the zone, though only for the spellcaster’s enemies.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell also gains the Drain damage effect.
- The spell may extend the mist out to one or more adjacent zones for 2 Success Levels per additional zone filled.

---

## **The Shape of Hate**

Devotees anointed with this blessing of Nyarlathotep are seized with battle fury and sprint straight at the enemy, desperate to get into melee and tear them to shreds.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell affects a number of allies within Close range equal to the spellcaster’s Power; this number may include or exclude the spellcaster at their discretion. When the spell is first cast, the affected characters immediately make a minor action to move toward the nearest enemy. In addition, affected allies inflict +2 damage on all melee attacks.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, when any affected ally makes a successful melee attack, they may make a second melee attack at +1 difficulty.

---

## **Touch of the Black Sun**

This spell projects a torrent of jet-black flames from the spellcaster’s hands directed at nearby enemies, causing their skin to writhe and burn.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets an enemy within Close range, inflicting Power +2 physical damage with the Area and Persistent 4 damage effects. The spellcaster cannot affect themselves with this spell.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense damage effect.
