# **Compendium of Mormo**

Mormo, often associated with fertility, growth, and natural abominations, inspires magic of creation and transformation, often with chaotic and uncontrolled elements. Cultists often use spells that control and shape life in perverse and unnatural ways.

---

## **Acidic Transformation**

The spellcaster alters their own flesh into a corrosive substance, allowing them to inflict horrific injuries on those they come into contact with.

- **Skill:** Medicine
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 6 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Category:** Transmutation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster transforms their own flesh into a corrosive substance, inflicting damage on those they touch. The spellcaster’s unarmed attacks inflict +Power physical Stress with the Persistent 4 and Piercing 1 damage effects. Enemies who make melee attacks or grapple the spellcaster with their natural weapons or unarmed attacks suffer 3 physical Stress with the Piercing 1 effect.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell means the spellcaster’s body corrodes itself too; the spellcaster suffers 36 physical Stress with the Drain effect at the end of every turn while the spell remains active.
**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, damage inflicted by this spell gains the Vicious damage effect.

---

## **Black Goat’s Blessing**

The spellcaster channels the energy of Mormo, invigorating their allies and making them more formidable.

- **Skill:** Resilience
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster bestows the blessing of Mormo upon their allies. Choose a number of allies equal to the spellcaster’s Power within Close range, increasing their Armour resistance by +2 and giving them the ability to regenerate 2 Stress at the end of each turn.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell causes the recipients to mutate grotesquely, increasing their Complication range by 1.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, increase the Stress regeneration by an additional 2.
- For 1 Success Levels, the spell affects one additional ally for each Success Levels spent.

---

## **Call the Corpse Eaters**

The spellcaster focuses, piercing the thin veil between dimensions to summon ghoul mobs to aid them.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Routine (+2), plus 1 for each ghoul mob
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell summons one, two, or three ghouls to aid the spellcaster. If multiple ghouls are summoned, they act as a mob. The ghouls obey the spellcaster’s commands, though more demanding orders may require additional persuasion. The ghouls return to their realm when the spell ends.

**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell summons the ghouls, but they are not under the spellcaster’s control.

**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, summon one additional ghoul per Success Levels spent.

---

## **Foul Birthing**

The spellcaster targets a single allied NPC, infusing them with a latent spell that will trigger upon their defeat.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Transmutation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell infuses a single NPC with a latent transformation spell that triggers upon their defeat, spawning an insect swarm or a rat swarm. The summoned creatures disperse when the spell ends.

**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell may trigger prematurely, causing the NPC to transform before they are defeated. The GM may spend 2 Doom to cause the spell’s effect to trigger when they desire.

**Success Levels:** The spawned creatures are under the spellcaster’s control. This cost must be paid when the spell is cast.

---

## **Necrophagus**

The spellcaster calls upon dark magic to inflict harm on their enemies, gaining sustenance from their pain.

- **Skill:** Medicine
- **Difficulty:** Hard (-4)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Medium range, inflicting XXX physical Stress, where XXX is the spellcaster’s Power +2. The spellcaster or one of their allies within Close range can recover up to half the Stress inflicted by this attack.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell does not allow the spellcaster or an ally to recover Stress.
**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, the spell gains the Vicious damage effect.

---

## **Sacrificial Rebirth**

The spellcaster sacrifices a creature, using its life force to create a new monstrous being loyal to the caster.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Hard (-4)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 3
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster sacrifices a creature within Reach, its life force being consumed to summon a Foetid Young. The sacrifice must be either willing or completely immobile, no larger than Scale 0, and be a Trooper NPC, and they are slain instantly. The Foetid Young obeys the spellcaster’s commands until the end of the scene, after which it vanishes.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell summons the Foetid Young, but it is not under the spellcaster’s control.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the Foetid Young is summoned with +1 Brawn and +1 Resilience.

---

## **Whisper of the Woods**

The spellcaster imprints their will upon plant life, causing it to grow and change to obstruct enemies.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 3 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster controls plant life within Medium range, causing it to grow rapidly and ensnare enemies. Select a single zone within Long range. Enemies within the affected zone must make an Athletics test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0) or become restrained, unable to move until they make a successful Athletics test on their turn.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell causes the plant life to grow out of control, affecting any creatures within the area, not only enemies.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, affect one additional zone within range. This option may be used a maximum number of times equal to the spellcaster’s Power.
