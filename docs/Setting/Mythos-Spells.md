# Mythos Spells

# **Tome of Cthulhu**

The Great Old One Cthulhu’s mind is so elemental that even while he dreams, his followers can use the power inherent in his thoughts, shaping his arcane visions to bring about change. Based on water magic, Cthulhu’s spell tome is primarily used by his children, the Deep Ones, spawn of Father Dagon and Mother Hydra. Mortal spellcasters who worship the Great Old One may also draw upon these incantations to summon the power of the unwholesome deep.

---
## **Call of the Deep**

Just as the ocean depths call to creatures swimming near the surface, beckoning them deeper, this spell awakens a powerful urge that compels those affected to move toward the nearest body of water, heedless of the harm it might cause them.

- **Skill:** Resilience
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0), opposed by target’s Willpower + Resilience
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Curse spell


**Spell Effect:** This spell targets a single living creature within Medium range. If the target is affected, it moves toward the nearest large body of water it can see, at the GM’s discretion as to whether a body of water is large enough. The only actions the target may take are moving toward that body of water and trying to break free of the spell, requiring a Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0). If the target reaches the water, it enters and dives downward until it either breaks free of the spell or drowns. If the target cannot see a body of water, it may act normally, as if unaware of the spell’s effects, but the spell remains in effect until it breaks free.

**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the difficulty of any skill test to break free of the spell increases by 1. This is not repeatable.
- For 2 Success Levels, the target moves toward the nearest large body of water whether or not it can see it.

## **Curse of Cthulhu**

The spellcaster calls on Cthulhu’s dreams made flesh and projects strange portents and terrible visions of the god who sleeps in the city of R’lyeh, sowing terror and confusion in their enemies’ ranks. Individuals and units affected by the curse become terrified and disoriented, losing focus on the task at hand and suffering significant damage to their willingness to fight.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** All enemies within Close range suffer XXX mental Stress, where XXX equals the spellcaster’s Power +1, with the Piercing 1 and Stun damage effects.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures within range, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, add the Persistent 4 damage effect to the damage; the persistent damage also has the Stun effect.

---

## **Echo of the Ocean**

The spellcaster calls forth an evocation of the ocean’s limitless depths to strengthen and reinforce their allies. Anyone imbued with this mighty force feels the raw, surging energy of the waves channeled through them, boosting their strength, performance, and morale until they feel nigh on unstoppable, ready to crash upon their foes like a mighty tsunami.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Routine (+2)
- **Cost:** 2 Drain
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster and all allies within Close range inflict +1 Stress on all their attacks, melee, ranged, and mental, and gain +2 Morale.

---

## **Gift of Cthulhu**

Using this spell, the spellcaster can temporarily take on the properties of a Deep One, even if they do not have any of the Deep Ones’ blood. Eyes bulge and become lidless, gill slits emerge from their neck, webbing appears between their fingers, and their feet elongate and take on a paddle-like quality. They can swim and breathe in the depths as if they were a partially transformed Deep One-human hybrid. Although the transformation is striking, the effects are not usually permanent, though a rare few suffer a profound longing to return to the comfort of the deeps.

- **Skill:** Survival
-  **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0); if targeted at an unwilling victim, opposed by the target’s Willpower + Resilience (Difficulty Challenging (+0))
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** A number of days equal to the spellcaster’s Power, or earlier if the spellcaster chooses to end the effect
- **Category:** Transmutation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets either the spellcaster or a single human within Reach. The target transforms, gaining the truth Deep One transmutation, representing their ability to breathe underwater, their ease at moving underwater, and their peculiar physical appearance. The target also gains an Athletics focus in Swimming. In addition, the character gains the Nauseating ability. When the spell’s effects end, unwilling targets must roll to see if they develop a scar, as if they had recovered from a mental injury.
**Success Levels:**
- If cast upon an unwilling victim, for 2 Success Levels the target also suffers 4 Piercing 2 mental damage.
- If cast upon the spellcaster themselves, or a willing target, for 2 Success Levels they may choose to suppress their transmutation for periods of up to an hour to allow them to hide without ending the effect.

---

## **Pool of Limpid Bliss**

Utilizing this unholy spell, the spellcaster causes a beautiful-looking pool to form that gleams and glistens, containing beautiful bathing water, nymphs, and handsome satyrs. Any individual who passes within Close range of the pool’s seductive area of effect must try to resist its watery allure. If they fail, they throw down their weapons and attempt to bathe in its glorious waters, floating there before sinking to drown in liquid ecstasy.

- **Skill:** Resilience
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** Choose a zone within Medium range; a pool of water, woven with spells of attraction, appears in that zone. Enemies in that zone at the start of their turn must attempt a Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0). Failure means they suffer 4 mental Stress with the Stun damage effect, and must move into the pool if not already within it. If stunned, they still move into the pool, compelled by the spell’s effects. A character who is Defeated by mental Stress caused by the pool will submerge themselves in the pool and begin Dying as they allow themselves to drown.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, increase the difficulty of all tests to resist the spell’s effects by 1.
- For 1 Success Level, increase the Stress the spell inflicts by +1 per Success Level spent.
- For 2 Success Levels, add the Piercing 1 effect.

---

## **Swimmer in the Elder Current**

Harnessing this watery enchantment, the spellcaster transforms themselves and any nearby allies into a purely liquid form. While in this state they are impervious to all damage and can flow according to the spellcaster’s will, escaping immediate harm and slipping away from danger.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Stun
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell may affect the spellcaster and any of the spellcaster’s allies within Close range, up to a number of targets equal to half the spellcaster’s Power, rounding up. The affected characters transform into water and flow away, reappearing moments later at any point within sight within Long range. Characters moved in this manner cannot take actions in their following turn, as if affected by the Stun effect.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, all characters transported by the spell receive +2 Cover until the start of the spellcaster’s next turn.
- For 2 Success Levels, all characters transported by the spell other than the spellcaster recover Stress equal to the spellcaster’s Survival skill.

---

## **Wave Storm**

Summoning a maelstrom of spinning, unpredictable liquid fury, the spellcaster projects a column of tempestuous water at their foes, enveloping them in watery doom. The vortex is short-lived and unpredictable, blown this way and that as if by an ocean storm, but any caught in its embrace will often have breathed their last.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures a vortex of water into a zone within Medium range. The vortex receives its own turn in the action order, but it cannot be attacked or suffer damage in any way. At the start of the vortex’s turn, it inflicts physical Stress equal to the spellcaster’s Power +1, with the Piercing 1 and Snare damage effects on all creatures and damageable objects within Close range of it, before moving to an adjacent zone, carrying any creatures it ensnares with it. Each time the vortex moves, the spellcaster may choose the direction if they are within Medium range of it. If the spellcaster is further away from the vortex than that, then the vortex remains stationary. The GM may spend 1 Doom to move the vortex instead when the spellcaster is further than Medium range from it.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell does not allow the spellcaster to control the vortex and does not require the GM to spend any Doom to move the vortex.
**Success Levels:** The spellcaster may increase the Stress the vortex deals by +1 per Success Levels spent.

# **Compendium of Mormo**

Mormo, often associated with fertility, growth, and natural abominations, inspires magic of creation and transformation, often with chaotic and uncontrolled elements. Cultists often use spells that control and shape life in perverse and unnatural ways.

---

## **Acidic Transformation**

The spellcaster alters their own flesh into a corrosive substance, allowing them to inflict horrific injuries on those they come into contact with.

- **Skill:** Medicine
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 6 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Category:** Transmutation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster transforms their own flesh into a corrosive substance, inflicting damage on those they touch. The spellcaster’s unarmed attacks inflict +Power physical Stress with the Persistent 4 and Piercing 1 damage effects. Enemies who make melee attacks or grapple the spellcaster with their natural weapons or unarmed attacks suffer 3 physical Stress with the Piercing 1 effect.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell means the spellcaster’s body corrodes itself too; the spellcaster suffers 36 physical Stress with the Drain effect at the end of every turn while the spell remains active.
**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, damage inflicted by this spell gains the Vicious damage effect.

---

## **Black Goat’s Blessing**

The spellcaster channels the energy of Mormo, invigorating their allies and making them more formidable.

- **Skill:** Resilience
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster bestows the blessing of Mormo upon their allies. Choose a number of allies equal to the spellcaster’s Power within Close range, increasing their Armour resistance by +2 and giving them the ability to regenerate 2 Stress at the end of each turn.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell causes the recipients to mutate grotesquely, increasing their Complication range by 1.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, increase the Stress regeneration by an additional 2.
- For 1 Success Levels, the spell affects one additional ally for each Success Levels spent.

---

## **Call the Corpse Eaters**

The spellcaster focuses, piercing the thin veil between dimensions to summon ghoul mobs to aid them.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Routine (+2), plus 1 for each ghoul mob
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell summons one, two, or three ghouls to aid the spellcaster. If multiple ghouls are summoned, they act as a mob. The ghouls obey the spellcaster’s commands, though more demanding orders may require additional persuasion. The ghouls return to their realm when the spell ends.

**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell summons the ghouls, but they are not under the spellcaster’s control.

**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, summon one additional ghoul per Success Levels spent.

---

## **Foul Birthing**

The spellcaster targets a single allied NPC, infusing them with a latent spell that will trigger upon their defeat.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Transmutation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell infuses a single NPC with a latent transformation spell that triggers upon their defeat, spawning an insect swarm or a rat swarm. The summoned creatures disperse when the spell ends.

**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell may trigger prematurely, causing the NPC to transform before they are defeated. The GM may spend 2 Doom to cause the spell’s effect to trigger when they desire.

**Success Levels:** The spawned creatures are under the spellcaster’s control. This cost must be paid when the spell is cast.

---

## **Necrophagus**

The spellcaster calls upon dark magic to inflict harm on their enemies, gaining sustenance from their pain.

- **Skill:** Medicine
- **Difficulty:** Hard (-4)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Medium range, inflicting XXX physical Stress, where XXX is the spellcaster’s Power +2. The spellcaster or one of their allies within Close range can recover up to half the Stress inflicted by this attack.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell does not allow the spellcaster or an ally to recover Stress.
**Success Levels:** For 1 Success Levels, the spell gains the Vicious damage effect.

---

## **Sacrificial Rebirth**

The spellcaster sacrifices a creature, using its life force to create a new monstrous being loyal to the caster.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Hard (-4)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 3
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Summoning spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster sacrifices a creature within Reach, its life force being consumed to summon a Foetid Young. The sacrifice must be either willing or completely immobile, no larger than Scale 0, and be a Trooper NPC, and they are slain instantly. The Foetid Young obeys the spellcaster’s commands until the end of the scene, after which it vanishes.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell summons the Foetid Young, but it is not under the spellcaster’s control.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the Foetid Young is summoned with +1 Brawn and +1 Resilience.

---

## **Whisper of the Woods**

The spellcaster imprints their will upon plant life, causing it to grow and change to obstruct enemies.

- **Skill:** Survival
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 3 Drain
- **Duration:** Until the end of the current scene
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster controls plant life within Medium range, causing it to grow rapidly and ensnare enemies. Select a single zone within Long range. Enemies within the affected zone must make an Athletics test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0) or become restrained, unable to move until they make a successful Athletics test on their turn.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell causes the plant life to grow out of control, affecting any creatures within the area, not only enemies.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, affect one additional zone within range. This option may be used a maximum number of times equal to the spellcaster’s Power.

# **Grimoire of Nyarlathotep**

The Messenger of the Outer Gods confers ominous boons upon his children and worshippers, sowing illusion, deception, delusion, and death. Those worshippers who wield Nyarlathotep’s unholy magics do so at great cost to their own personal health and rationality.

Drawn from the power of the Dreamlands, the battlefield spells contained in the Grimoire of Nyarlathotep are among the foulest sorceries practiced by any faction in the Hidden Wars. The Crawling Chaos delights to see them inflicted upon friend and foe alike.

---

## **Delirium of the Dark Pharaoh**

Tapping into the sprawling chaos of the Dreamlands, the spellcaster calls forth the power of the Crawling Chaos, conjuring horrific visions and glimpses of strange dimensions and eerie planes beyond this earthly existence.

Characters find their willpower stretched to breaking point, and any who are unable to resist are gripped in an unearthly fervor. Delirious, they turn against each other or nearby allies until their temporary frenzy abates.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single creature within Medium range. The target suffers Power +1 mental Stress with the Persistent 5 damage effect. If this spell would otherwise inflict one or more injuries on its target, either from the mental damage dealt or from the Persistent damage effect, then instead of sustaining injuries, the target is driven to a violent frenzy and attacks the nearest creature, friend or foe, without thought of pain or danger. This frenzy ends at the end of the scene or until the target is Defeated.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, both the initial damage roll and subsequent rolls required by the Persistent damage effect gain the Piercing 1 damage effect.
- For every 2 Success Levels spent, the spell affects one additional target.

---

## **Lesser Black Rain**

Adapted from ancient Hyperborean magic, this spell summons a swirling maelstrom of lightning-flecked, thunderous black clouds. Once the maelstrom has formed, the spell cannot be halted, and soon after, the vortex rains down razor-sharp shards of jagged obsidian on any victims not under hard cover.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster selects a zone they can perceive within Long range, and a menacing storm gathers above that zone. The storm cannot be attacked or damaged in any way, and it receives its own turn in the action order. The storm does nothing on its first turn after being created, but on each of its subsequent turns, it inflicts Power physical damage with the Piercing 1 damage effect on all creatures and damageable objects within its zone that are not within hard cover, a ceiling or object that fully impedes an attack.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, add the Vicious damage effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, move the storm to any adjacent zone as a major action, requiring a Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Challenging (+0).

---

## **Mask of the Faceless Sphinx**

Calling on the power of the Crawling Chaos, the spellcaster invokes blessings for their allies, who gain the ability to cloak themselves in the aspect of one of the God of a Thousand Forms’ most inscrutable incarnations, the Faceless Sphinx. While under this protection, their outlines seem to shift and shimmer, making them much harder to hit, while any damage they take is much reduced.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain, Persistent 3
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Blessing spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster and any allies within Close range when the spell is cast become harder to hit, increasing the difficulty of attacks against them by +1. Further, they count as having heavy cover.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the difficulty increase for attacks against the affected characters increases to +2.
- For 2 Success Levels, affected characters receive +4 Cover resistance instead of the +2 Cover resistance normally granted by heavy cover.
- For 3 Success Levels, any ranged or melee attack that misses an affected character inflicts 4 mental damage with the Stun damage effect upon the attacker.

---

## **Rays of the Black Sun (Lesser Dread Equinox)**

Evoking the power of chaos and confusion, the spellcaster draws down and directs a scintilla of the rays of the black sun directly at the enemy.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 1
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Medium range. The target suffers Power +2 mental damage with the Piercing 1 and Snare damage effects.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Persistent 4 damage effect.
- The spell may affect one additional target for every 2 Success Levels spent.

## **The Nameless Mists**

The spellcaster summons a small portion of the Dark Pharaoh’s unholy sibling, the Nameless Mist, to Earth. There, it manifests as a restless, shifting, chaotic mist, lingering and burning with a horrible internal intensity, damaging anyone caught in its unholy tendrils.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** A number of rounds equal to the spellcaster’s Power
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures a foul mist that fills the spellcaster’s current zone. Any enemy within the mist at the end of their turn suffers mental damage equal to the spellcaster’s Power +1, with the Piercing 2 and Stun damage effects. In addition, the zone gains a location truth, Shifting, Vile Mists, that hinders vision into, out of, and through the zone, though only for the spellcaster’s enemies.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell also gains the Drain damage effect.
- The spell may extend the mist out to one or more adjacent zones for 2 Success Levels per additional zone filled.

## **The Shape of Hate**

Devotees anointed with this blessing of Nyarlathotep are seized with battle fury and sprint straight at the enemy, desperate to get into melee and tear them to shreds.

- **Skill:** Academia

- **Category:** Blessing spell

When the spell is first cast, the affected characters immediately make a minor action to move toward the nearest enemy. In addition, affected allies inflict +2 damage on all melee attacks.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, when any affected ally makes a successful melee attack, they may make a second melee attack at +1 difficulty.

- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets an enemy within Close range, inflicting Power +2 physical damage with the Area and Persistent 4 damage effects. The spellcaster cannot affect themselves with this spell.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense damage effect.

# **Spellbook of Yog-Sothoth**

Yog-Sothoth, the Outer God, is both keeper of all knowledge and coterminous with all time and space, and so its magic is based on unlocking gateways, freeing forbidden knowledge, and revealing the hidden pathways that open believers’ eyes to the true nature of reality. Unlike Nyarlathotep, Yog-Sothoth is not malign in the traditional sense, but welcomes those who would gaze upon the true state of the multiverse, even if it might drive them insane. Even though one aspect of it is trapped with the black sun, the god sometimes manifests itself as a succession of gleaming vortexes and spheres. Occasionally it has been known to interfere in the affairs of mortals, impregnating them to create monstrous hybrids.

---

## **Binding of Yog-Sothoth**

Calling on the chaotic aspects of the Outer God to confuse and mislead, the sorcerer binds all enemies nearby with this spell. While it does no direct physical damage, those who succumb to its deleterious effects are overcome, becoming rooted to the spot, unable to move, attack, or defend themselves.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Control spell

**Spell Effect:** All enemies within Close range suffer Power +2 mental Stress with the Snare effect. Enemies who would suffer one or more injuries from this spell are paralyzed instead of suffering injuries. Paralyzed enemies may not take any action other than to try to break free, requiring a successful Willpower + Resilience test with a difficulty of Difficult (-2).
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures within Close range, not just enemies.

**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 1 effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Persistent 3 damage effect; the Persistent damage also has the Snare effect.

---

## **Foetid Touch of Aforgomon**

Sometimes the Outer God can be persuaded to “favor” mortals with its direct touch, impregnating them with its unholy seed. Some may be able to resist these foul effects, but if possessed, they slowly begin to transform into the fiendish Bloodborn.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2), opposed by the target’s Agility + Fighting (Difficulty Difficult (-2))
- **Cost:** 7 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets a single enemy within Reach. If the spell is successfully cast, the target suffers Power +1 physical Stress with the Persistent 3 effect. Any character Defeated by this spell immediately starts dying, and if they die, a Bloodborn emerges from the character’s remains in the following round.

**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect, applied to both the initial Stress and any Stress caused by the Persistent effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense effect, which applies to both the initial Stress and any caused by the Persistent effect.

---

## **Gaze of the Multiverse**

Yog-Sothoth is the key and the gate, the keeper of hidden knowledge. Using this spell, the spellcaster opens the victim’s eyes to the true nature of the cold, harsh, unyielding universe, which has the nuclear chaos of the mad, blind god Azathoth at its center. Those affected by Gaze of the Multiverse are overcome by the overwhelming nature of this revelation and lose coherence and focus for its duration.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Difficult (-2)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** All enemies within Close range of the spellcaster suffer Power +2 mental Stress, with the Stun effect.
**Flawed:** A flawed version of this spell affects all creatures within Close range, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Drain effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Vicious effect.

---

## **Loathsome Bond of 'Umr At-Tawil**

Yog-Sothoth is the warden of forbidden knowledge and this fearsome spell is aimed at breaking a foe’s morale. Using this loathsome incantation, the sorcerer not only does substantial harm, but siphons off a portion of their foe’s life force to replenish their own.

- **Skill:** Persuasion
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 4 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell targets each enemy within Close range and inflicts Power +1 mental Stress with the Drain and Piercing 1 effects. In addition, if the spell inflicts one or more injuries, the spellcaster may remove one of their own injuries.
**Flawed:** If a flawed version of this spell is cast, it targets every creature within Close range, not just enemies.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Intense effect.
- For 2 Success Levels, the spellcaster removes one injury for each injury inflicted.

---

## **Way of the Gatekeeper**

Yog-Sothoth is the opener of the ways, and guardian of the hidden paths, and casters are able to draw on this forbidden knowledge to move themselves and their allies swiftly and silently across the battlefield, sometimes appearing deep behind enemy lines, or using these hidden dimensional byways to evade imminent danger.

- **Skill:** Academia
- **Difficulty:** Challenging (+0)
- **Cost:** 5 Drain, Piercing 2
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Manifestation spell

**Spell Effect:** The spellcaster transports a number of willing allies in Close range equal to half their Power, rounding up; this may include or exclude themselves. The affected characters vanish and reappear in any zone within Long range.
**Success Levels:**
- For 2 Success Levels, the maximum number of affected allies is equal to the spellcaster’s Power, rather than only half.
- For 2 Success Levels, the affected characters may reappear in a location within a mile which the spellcaster is familiar with, provided they have spent at least an hour in that location previously.

---

## **Spheres of Yog-Sothoth**

The sorcerer summons a small portion of Yog-Sothoth’s being to this plane, in the form of a series of ephemeral spheres glowing with a pallid unholy light, then directs them at their enemies who wilt under the Outer God’s unclean touch.

- **Skill:** Fighting
- **Difficulty:** Varies, see below
- **Cost:** 5 Drain. In addition, each Effect rolled on the cost adds 1 to Doom.
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Category:** Attack spell

**Spell Effect:** The spell conjures one, two, or three spheres, which the spellcaster hurls at enemies within Medium range. The difficulty of the spell is equal to the number of spheres summoned. Each sphere targets a different enemy, and each enemy hit suffers Power +2 physical Stress with the Drain and Stun effects.
**Success Levels:** For 2 Success Levels, the spell gains the Piercing 2 effect.
